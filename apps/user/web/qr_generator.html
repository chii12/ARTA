ARTA Survey QR Code Generator

Sample Survey URLs:

Default Survey:
https://yourapp.com/survey/default
Custom Survey:
https://yourapp.com/survey/custom123

How to use:

  1. Generate a QR code using the form above
  2. Open the ARTA User app on your phone
  3. Tap "Scan QR Code" button
  4. Point your camera at the QR code
  5. The survey will open automatically

Alternative: You can also tap "Enter Survey URL" in the app and paste the URL directly.
